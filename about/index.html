About Me

Interests

Hi! My name is Sylvester Dzimiri. I am a Electrical Engineering and Computer Science graduate. I am a highly motivated, curious, and fast self-learner adept at synthesizing ideas, methods, and tools from multiple fields to make high-impact contributions in domains ranging from numerical methods to workflow processes. I have a strong interest in software development, problem modeling and analysis, software design and testing, software verification and validation, software process, and software management. I am a strong believer in continuous learning, I am also language agnostic and interested in learning new technologies. I am interested in projects in which my analytical skills, innovative thinking, and ability to distill essential information from complex systems are used to tackle challenging practical problems.
